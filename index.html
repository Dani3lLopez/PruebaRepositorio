About Us

About our Team

Located in Central America, our bilingual Virtual Assistants are hired based on a thorough selection process.

Each prospect's proficiency is measured through a variety of scenarios including an english proficiency test, written and verbal tasks during a set of interviews and training, and finally tested on the phones during their nesting period.

Acquisitions - Closers

Once we have determined the seller’s interest and motivation to sell, our Acquisitions Agents will find comparable properties in the area to calculate the ARV in order to present and negotiate an offer.
